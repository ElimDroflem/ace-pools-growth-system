Alex's operating guide

Follow this sequence to start winning customers.

Do not wait for every future feature. The first objective is five completed Pool Reviews and five annual PoolCare customers.

Before contacting anyone

Every working day

09:00

Choose ten leads

Open the dashboard. Prioritise QR responses, suitable former customers and strong public signals.

09:30

Make contact

Use the correct script. Lead with safety, running cost and the complimentary Pool Review.

12:00

Book and confirm

Offer two appointment times. Ask for equipment photos and recent pool energy costs where available.

16:30

Close the loop

Send reports and quotes. Every open lead receives a dated next action.

From lead to customer

  1. Qualify: location, pool type, age, current care, main concern, decision-maker and timing.
  2. Book: confirm a Pool Review appointment and send the preparation message.
  3. Inspect: complete the review form, take evidence photographs and identify the three most important actions.
  4. Recommend: explain how PoolCare Complete improves safety confidence, prevents waste and removes ongoing worry.
  5. Quote: send the annual-plan quotation within 48 hours and follow up the next day.
  6. Onboard: collect access, equipment, payment and communication details; set the visit day.
  7. Prove value: send a clear report after the first service and log future upgrades.
